Who We Are?

Welcome to The Cake Shop Bakery, Tallahassee’s best bakery. The Cake Shop Bakery is a family owned and operated business in Tallahassee. We carefully select our ingredients for their freshness, flavor and natural value. We choose organic, locally grown products when available. Everything we make embodies our commitment to consistency and excellence. The heart of the bakery is a small, dedicated group of hard working bakers. We are grateful to our amazing staff, loyal customers, and the most supportive local community who allow us to do the work we love every day.


Grab a bite, take a seat, and enjoy all we have to offer.

Meet Your Bakers

Linda Richards is the owner and magic behind all the delicious treats. Growing up with a chef as a father who cooked for their family of six, Linda has always had a passion for baking. She has brought many family recipes to the bakery’s repertoire including her Monkey Pies, Oatmeal Raisin Cookies, and her famous Chocolate Chip Cookies “just like Grandma used to make.”
